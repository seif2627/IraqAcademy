Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

At Iraq Academy, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and share information to operate the platform.

2. Data We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Account and contact details (full name, email, phone number).
  • Required profile information (date of birth, ID number, full address, city, governorate).
  • Course and enrollment activity (courses viewed, enrolled, or completed).

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to manage your account, operate courses and enrollments, provide support, and communicate important service updates.

4. Services We Use

To deliver the platform, we rely on the following service providers:

  • Firebase Authentication for account sign-in and verification emails.
  • Convex for application database storage and backend operations.
  • Cloudflare for hosting and content delivery.
  • SMTP email delivery for system notifications.

If payments are enabled in the future, Stripe will be used for payment processing and will be disclosed here before activation.

5. Notifications

We send system emails for account verification, password reset, and enrollment updates. Admin or teacher notifications may include limited student identifiers such as name or email to coordinate enrollments.

6. Data Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, modification, or disclosure.

7. Cookies & Tracking

We do not use analytics or advertising cookies at this time. Essential cookies or local storage may be used to keep you signed in and maintain basic session state.

8. Data Requests & Account Deletion

If you would like to request a copy of your data or delete your account, contact support and we will assist you. Account deletion is currently handled by support upon request.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via the support page or email at iraqacademy@mesopost.com.
