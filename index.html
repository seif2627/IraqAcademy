How IraqAcademy works

1. Student submits their details

The student fills a simple registration form with real contact information and Telegram handle. In this demo nothing is stored; it only updates the on-page dashboard.

2. Admin creates and activates codes

Admins log in to a simple console, generate random codes, and mark them as activated once payment is confirmed or the student joins the right Telegram group.

Frequently Asked Questions

+ Is this a real, live platform?

This is a demo-only prototype. It shows how the flows would look, but there is no real database or Telegram bot connected yet.

+ Are any students or phone numbers real?

All names, numbers and Telegram handles on this site are fake. They are here only to make the prototype feel like a real product.

+ How will Telegram activation work later?

In a production build, codes would be validated through a Telegram bot. When a student sends a valid code, the bot adds them to the right group and marks the code as activated.

+ What backend will we use?

The plan is to use Supabase for authentication, storage and simple dashboards, keeping the whole stack modern and easy to extend.
